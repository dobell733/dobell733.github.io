About Me


Hello, I'm Dominic. It's a pleasure to meet you. I'm a software engineer with a passion for writing clean, elegant, and efficient code. While I have previous experience in the administrative side of the health care field, my journey took an exciting turn in early 2021 when I discovered my love for software development.


I dedicated about a year to self-learning on Codecademy, immersing myself in various programming languages and concepts. In March 2022, I decided to take my skills to the next level and officially enrolled in the Post-Baccalaureate Computer Science Program at Oregon State University.


I invite you to explore my portfolio and check out the projects I've worked on. I'm eager to connect with fellow enthusiasts and collaborate on exciting opportunities. Let's create something amazing together!
